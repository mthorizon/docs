Welcome

Sequence Builder is a comprehensive web3 game development hub that serves as your entry point to the Sequence platform. From Sequence Builder you can manage your projects, obtain API keys, configure wallets, deploy contracts, customize your own marketplace and more. Available through your favorite engine or framework like Unity, Unreal, Typescript or React Native to build on nearly any platform.

To learn more about how to leverage the powerful features that Sequence has to offer, check out the pages below:

Enable users to link their embedded wallet in your game with an external wallet - allowing you to query assets, balances, and allow easy flow of liquidity to your game.
